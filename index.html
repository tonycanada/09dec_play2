Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PlayOn gaming platform

How does the skill-matching system work?

Our advanced algorithm analyzes your quiz game performance, leaderboard position, and tournament history to match you with players of similar skill levels. This ensures fair and competitive gaming experiences in all our tournaments.

Can I really win rewards on PlayOn?

Absolutely! Every tournament victory earns you points, badges, and exclusive rewards. Top performers on our global leaderboards unlock premium features and special recognition within our gaming community.

What types of quiz games are available?

We offer 50+ game categories including trivia, brain games, knowledge quizzes, esports challenges, and skill-based competitions. New games and tournaments are added daily to keep the experience fresh.

How much does it cost to play?

PlayOn is completely free to join and play most tournaments. We offer premium tiers for players seeking advanced features and exclusive gaming challenges, but basic access to our quiz hub is always free.

Is there a mobile app for PlayOn?

PlayOn is fully optimized for mobile browsers and works seamlessly on any device. We're currently developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android to enhance your gaming experience.

How can I improve my leaderboard ranking?

Play regularly in tournaments, focus on categories where you excel, and challenge yourself with higher difficulty skill games. Consistency and learning from each competition helps you climb the global leaderboards faster.
